How to Use BudgetTracker

BudgetTracker is a personal finance management platform designed to help users monitor income, expenses, savings, and long-term financial growth. The website provides multiple budgeting tools that allow users to create monthly budgets, analyze yearly performance, manage annual finances, and plan future financial goals.

Budget Dashboard

The Budget Dashboard serves as the central hub of the application. It provides quick access to all budgeting modules, including Monthly Budget, Year Budget, Annual Budget, Five-Year Budget, and Quick Budget.

Monthly Budget

The Monthly Budget module helps users track their income and expenses for a specific month. The system automatically calculates total income, expenses, savings, and saving rate.

Year Budget

The Year Budget module provides an overview of financial performance across the entire year and helps identify spending patterns and yearly trends.

Annual Budget

Users can enter annual income and expenses across various categories. The system generates financial insights, savings calculations, and visual charts.

Five-Year Budget

The Five-Year Budget module helps users plan future income, expenses, savings, and long-term financial growth over a five-year period.

Quick Budget

Quick Budget provides an instant financial overview by calculating savings and balance using simple income and expense values.

Restrictions & Requirements

Users can explore the website without logging in, but saving, viewing, and deleting budget records requires authentication and an active account.
